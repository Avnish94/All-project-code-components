What you'll need:

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast
  • Olive oil
  • Salt & Pepper

Materials:

  • Skillet (lid optional)
  • Spatula
  • Rolling pin
  • Gallon size ziplock bag


Health Info:

Not sure how we plan on formatting this, but I left room for it.

Cooking Instructions:

Step 1:

Defrost the chicken breast.

Step 2:

Put the chicken in the ziplock bag and flatten with the rolling pin so it cooks evenly.

Step 3:

Season the chicken.

Step 4:

Pour the olive oil into the pan & heat over the stove top. Once hot, add the chicken.

Step 5:

Flip the chicken once the bottom side is done.

Step 6:

Remove from skillet with the spatula, and enjoy.
